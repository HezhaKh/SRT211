Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mix.
  3. Add in the flour and mix untill combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 min.
